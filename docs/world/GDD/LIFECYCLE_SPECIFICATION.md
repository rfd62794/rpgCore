# rpgCore Aging & Lifecycle Specification
**Design Document v1.0 — Living World Engine**

---

## 🌱 Core Philosophy

A slime's life has arc. It is born, it grows, it reaches its peak, it ages into wisdom, and eventually it retires or is lost. The lifecycle creates emotional investment — you care about a slime more when you've watched it develop than when it appeared fully formed.

The lifecycle intersects with the genetic tier system to create different experiences at each combination. A Sundered slime aging into Prime is volatile and exciting. A Liminal slime reaching Elder with its one weakness exposed is poignant. A Void slime hatching at all is an event.

---

## 👶 Life Stages

### Hatchling — Levels 0-1
*"Just arrived. Still becoming."*

Culture identity forming but not fully expressed. Stats minimal. Visually: smaller, rounder, less defined than adult form. Expression states limited — mostly curiosity and contentment.

**Capabilities:**
- Garden only. Cannot be dispatched.
- No combat eligibility.
- Begins responding to environment — personality seeds here.
- Eating and resting animations establish character.

**Care Required:** Feeding. Attention. Warmth from nearby Elder slimes accelerates development.

---

### Juvenile — Levels 2-3
*"Learning what it is."*

Culture traits becoming visible in color and behavior. First dispatches eligible for low-risk zones. Carnival minigames unlock. Stat growth is fastest here — the learning phase.

**Capabilities:**
- Low-risk dispatch zones eligible.
- Carnival minigames unlock (stat training begins).
- Breeding not yet eligible.
- Equipment slots locked.

**Note for Tier 2 Bordered:** Juvenile phase slightly extended — two cultures negotiating expression takes longer to settle. But Prime phase is stronger for it.

**Note for Tier 3 Sundered:** Juvenile behavior is unpredictable. Stat spikes rather than smooth growth. The internal tension is most visible here.

---

### Young — Levels 4-5
*"Identity established. Ready for the world."*

Full culture expression established. Primary stat profile locked in. The slime is recognizably itself now. Breeding eligibility begins. Equipment slots open.

**Capabilities:**
- Full dispatch eligibility (most zones).
- Breeding eligible begins.
- Equipment slots open — class expression begins.
- Culture hub recognition (NPCs acknowledge culture identity).

**Tier 4 Bordered dispatch bonus:** Intersection zone dispatch unlocks at level 4 rather than 5 for adjacent dual breeds.

---

### Prime — Levels 6-7
*"The peak. This is what they were meant to be."*

Maximum stat growth rate. Best dispatch candidate. Breeding at this stage produces the strongest offspring. The core of any active roster. The player feels the most confident deploying Prime slimes.

**Capabilities:**
- All zones eligible including high-risk.
- Peak stat growth rate.
- Breeding at Prime produces optimal offspring.
- Full personality expression — behavior is consistent and readable.

**Tier 3 Sundered at Prime:** Volatile and exciting. High performance ceiling but internal tension still creates behavioral surprises. Worth managing carefully.

**Tier 6 Convergent at Prime:** Void characteristics more visible. Other slimes in the garden begin orienting toward them passively.

---

### Veteran — Levels 8-9
*"Still capable. Now wise."*

Growth slowing but wisdom accumulating. Mentoring younger slimes begins — passive stat bonuses to garden slimes they spend time near. Still dispatch eligible but the player starts weighing risk differently. Losing a Veteran feels different than losing a Young slime.

**Capabilities:**
- All zones still eligible.
- Mentoring bonus activates — affects nearby Hatchlings and Juveniles.
- Slower stat growth.
- Breeding still possible but offspring may show age influence in genetics.
- Risk calculus changes — the player learns to protect Veteran slimes.

**Permanent loss mechanic:** Veteran slimes lost on dangerous dispatches create genuine grief. This is intentional. The loss should mean something.

---

### Elder — Level 10
*"The garden remembers them."*

No longer dispatched ideally. Permanent garden resident. Mentoring bonus maximized. A slime reaching Elder has lived a full life in the player's care.

**Capabilities:**
- Dispatch strongly discouraged (still possible but the game signals this clearly).
- Maximum mentoring bonus — significant influence on Hatchlings nearby.
- Breeding at Elder produces rare outcomes. Accumulated genetic expression. Void probability increases slightly with Elder parents.
- Named Elders become part of garden history — appear in the garden's lore record.
- Unique visual novel conversation scenes available for Elders with sufficient relationship depth.

**Tier 7 Liminal at Elder:** The missing culture becomes a visible narrative presence. NPCs who visit the garden react. The absence is felt.

**Tier 8 Void at Elder:** A world event. Culture hub emissaries arrive. Garden resonates differently. Unique conversation branches open across all relationship tracks.

---

### Death & Legacy
*"They were here. That matters."*

Natural conclusion to a full life. The slime's legacy persists:

- Offspring carry their genetics forward.
- Named Elders remain in the garden's permanent lore record.
- Mentoring bonuses linger briefly as "remembered lessons" on slimes they trained.
- The player can view lineage trees showing where a slime's genetics live on.

**Permanent loss in dispatches:** Slimes lost in dungeons or high-risk missions are gone. No recovery. This creates real stakes for dispatch decisions and makes Veteran and Elder slimes precious. The garden feels different without them.

## 🧬 Tier × Lifecycle Interactions

### Tier 1 Blooded
Standard lifecycle. Full level range 0-10. Predictable and readable. Culture hub fully recognizes them at any level. The baseline experience.

### Tier 2 Bordered
Juvenile phase slightly extended — two cultures negotiating expression. Prime phase compensates with above-average peak performance.

### Tier 3 Sundered
Unpredictable leveling throughout. Stat spikes. Behavioral surprises. Highest risk, highest ceiling. Managing a Sundered slime from Juvenile to Prime is a gameplay story in itself.

### Tier 4 Drifted
Most stable leveling curve of all dual breeds. Consistent, reliable. What you breed when you need dependability over spectacle.

### Tier 5 Threaded
Mentoring bonus appears earlier than normal — complexity creates wisdom. Threaded slimes at Veteran begin influencing garden culture noticeably.

### Tier 6 Convergent
Slower early leveling — four cultures take longer to settle. Ceiling is higher. Elder stage unlocks unique garden interactions. Other slimes are drawn to them passively at all stages.

### Tier 7 Liminal
Accelerated leveling once past Juvenile — the near-complete genetic picture wants to resolve quickly. Elder stage is narratively charged. The missing culture becomes a presence by its absence.

### Tier 8 Void
Cannot hatch naturally in the wild. Garden-exclusive birth condition. Leveling is smooth and balanced — no culture is fighting for dominance. Visually distinct at every age stage. Elder Void slimes are world events.

## 📈 Generation Depth as Lifecycle Modifier

Generation is recorded in the genome and influences lifecycle expression:

- **Gen 1-2:** Standard lifecycle. What you find in the world.
- **Gen 3-5:** Genetics becoming refined. Visual clarity increases. Personality more defined.
- **Gen 6-9:** Deliberate breeding showing. Stats more consistent within expected ranges. Throwback patterns may emerge.
- **Gen 10+:** Lineage visible in the slime's appearance. Deep history. Void proximity increases measurably.

---

## 📚 The Garden Lore Record

Every Elder slime that reaches level 10 earns a permanent entry in the garden's lore record. This is a persistent document the player can browse — names, lineage, notable dispatches, relationships formed, offspring produced.

The lore record is the garden's memory. It outlasts individual slimes. It makes the garden feel inhabited rather than managed.

Named Elders whose offspring go on to significant achievements get updated entries. The record grows as the garden grows. Losing the last descendant of a notable lineage is a real loss — that branch of the tree ends.

## 🏗️ ECS Integration Specifications

### Lifecycle Components
```python
@dataclass
class LifecycleComponent:
    """Lifecycle state and progression"""
    level: int
    experience: int
    stage: LifecycleStage  # HATCHLING, JUVENILE, YOUNG, PRIME, VETERAN, ELDER
    age_days: int
    total_dispatches: int
    successful_dispatches: int
    
    # Stage-specific capabilities
    can_dispatch: bool
    can_breed: bool
    can_equip: bool
    mentoring_bonus: float
    
    def advance_stage(self) -> bool:
        """Advance to next lifecycle stage if conditions met"""
        
    def get_stage_capabilities(self) -> StageCapabilities:
        """Get current stage capabilities"""

@dataclass
class MentoringComponent:
    """Mentoring relationships and bonuses"""
    mentor_id: Optional[UUID]  # Elder slime mentoring this one
    mentees: List[UUID]  # Younger slimes being mentored
    mentoring_strength: float  # Based on mentor's stage and generation
    remembered_lessons: List[str]  # Legacy bonuses from deceased mentors
    
    def apply_mentoring_bonus(self, target_stats: dict) -> dict:
        """Apply mentoring stat bonuses"""
        
    def add_mentee(self, mentee_id: UUID) -> None:
        """Add a younger slime to mentor"""

@dataclass
class LoreRecordComponent:
    """Garden lore and history tracking"""
    is_named: bool
    name: Optional[str]
    notable_achievements: List[str]
    offspring_achievements: List[str]
    garden_history_entries: List[str]
    relationship_depth: Dict[str, float]  # Bond with player and other slimes
    
    def add_achievement(self, achievement: str) -> None:
        """Add notable achievement to lore record"""
        
    def update_garden_history(self, event: str) -> None:
        """Add event to garden history"""
```

### Lifecycle Systems
```python
class LifecycleSystem:
    """Manages lifecycle progression and stage transitions"""
    def add_experience(self, entity: Entity, amount: int) -> bool:
        """Add experience and check for level up"""
        
    def advance_lifecycle_stage(self, entity: Entity) -> bool:
        """Advance to next lifecycle stage"""
        
    def calculate_stage_capabilities(self, entity: Entity) -> StageCapabilities:
        """Calculate capabilities based on stage and tier"""

class MentoringSystem:
    """Manages mentoring relationships between slimes"""
    def assign_mentor(self, mentor: Entity, mentee: Entity) -> bool:
        """Assign Elder mentor to younger slime"""
        
    def apply_mentoring_effects(self, entity: Entity) -> None:
        """Apply mentoring bonuses from nearby Elders"""
        
    def handle_mentor_death(self, mentor: Entity) -> None:
        """Convert active mentoring to remembered lessons"""

class LoreSystem:
    """Manages garden lore and history"""
    def record_achievement(self, entity: Entity, achievement: str) -> None:
        """Record notable achievement"""
        
    def update_garden_lore(self, event: str) -> None:
        """Update garden-wide lore record"""
        
    def generate_lore_display(self, entity: Entity) -> LoreDisplay:
        """Generate lore display for UI"""
```

## 📊 Performance Specifications

### Memory Usage
- **Per Slime**: ~200 bytes for lifecycle data
- **Population**: 100 slimes = ~20KB lifecycle data
- **Lore Records**: Variable, average ~500 bytes per named slime

### Computation Requirements
- **Experience Processing**: <0.1ms per slime
- **Stage Advancement**: <0.5ms per transition
- **Mentoring Calculations**: <0.2ms per mentor-mentee pair
- **Lore Updates**: <1ms per achievement
